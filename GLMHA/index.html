Welcome to the Great Lakes Morgan Family!

Whether you're a lifelong Morgan enthusiast, a new owner finding your footing, a dedicated breeder shaping the future, a talented trainer honing skills, or simply someone captivated by the beauty and spirit of America's first breed, you've found your community here! The Great Lakes Morgan Horse Association is more than just an organization; we're a passionate group of friends, mentors, and advocates united by our love for these incredible horses.

Our mission is simple: to celebrate, promote, and preserve the versatile Morgan horse throughout Michigan, Ohio, and the surrounding Great Lakes states. We strive to be your most trusted and comprehensive resource, connecting you with reputable farms and trainers through our detailed Directory, keeping you informed about regional Events, and showcasing exceptional Morgan Horses For Sale in our area.

Above all, we aim to foster a warm, supportive network that encourages education, good sportsmanship, and lasting camaraderie among all who appreciate the Morgan's unique ability to excel in nearly any discipline and capture hearts with its intelligence and people-loving nature. Explore our site, connect with fellow members, and discover the enduring legacy of the Morgan horse. We're so glad you're here!
